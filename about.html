About Me

I am a mechanical engineering student with a strong interest in automatic control, robotics and probabilistic machine learning. I enjoy working at the interface between physical systems, data and algorithms.

Who I Am

My background combines classical mechanical engineering with modern control and data-driven methods. Through my bachelor and master studies, I have worked on projects ranging from experimental mechanics and measurement setups to embedded control and probabilistic modelling.

What motivates me is taking a real physical system – a robot, a flexible mast, a rotating shaft or a falling disk – and building the full chain from modelling and sensing to algorithms and implementation.

Technical Interests

Control & Robotics

Modelling and control of dynamical systems, implementation of feedback algorithms on microcontrollers, and robotics projects involving sensors, actuators and real-time decision making.

Probabilistic ML & Decision Making

Gaussian processes, Bayesian optimization and approximate Bayesian inference to make decisions under uncertainty, especially when experiments are costly or safety-critical.

Experimental Mechanics & Measurement

Designing experimental setups, integrating sensors, building acquisition pipelines and processing signals to understand the behaviour of mechanical structures in practice.

How I Work

In my projects, I like to start from the physics and constraints of the real system, then design a clean and modular approach: from modelling and simulation to experimental validation and data analysis.

I pay attention to clear documentation, reproducible code and readable figures, whether I am working alone or in a team. I enjoy discussing ideas, challenging assumptions and iterating quickly between theory and experiments.

Outside of Engineering

Outside of my studies, I enjoy activities that keep me curious and active. I like exploring new technical topics, learning tools that help me build better experiments and systems, and spending time outdoors to disconnect from the screen.

What I Am Looking For

I am interested in opportunities related to control, robotics and data-driven modelling, especially projects that involve both experimental setups and algorithmic development.
